How to Back Out QST from a Quebec Invoice

Quebec is the only province that applies two separate taxes — federal GST (5%) and provincial QST (9.975%) — each calculated independently. The combined rate is 14.975%. When you have a tax-included total and need to work backwards to the pre-tax amount, you need the reverse formula.

The Reverse GST + QST Formula (Quebec 14.975%)

The formula is straightforward:

Pre-tax amount = Total ÷ 1.14975

Quebec's two taxes break down as follows:

  • GST rate: 5% (federal)
  • QST rate: 9.975% (provincial — applied to pre-tax amount, not on top of GST)
  • Combined rate: 14.975%
  • Formula: Pre-tax = Total ÷ 1.14975
  • Total tax: Total − Pre-tax

Unlike some provinces where PST is calculated on the GST-included amount, Quebec's QST is applied to the pre-tax price only, so the combined divisor is simply 1.14975.

Example 1 — $11.50 total: $11.50 ÷ 1.14975 = $10.00 pre-tax. GST: $10.00 × 0.05 = $0.50. QST: $10.00 × 0.09975 = $1.00. Total tax: $1.50.

Example 2 — $57.49 total: $57.49 ÷ 1.14975 = $50.00 pre-tax. GST: $50.00 × 0.05 = $2.50. QST: $50.00 × 0.09975 = $4.99. Total tax: $7.49.

Example 3 — $114.98 total: $114.98 ÷ 1.14975 = $100.00 pre-tax. GST: $100.00 × 0.05 = $5.00. QST: $100.00 × 0.09975 = $9.98. Total tax: $14.98.

Example 4 — $287.43 total: $287.43 ÷ 1.14975 = $250.00 pre-tax. GST: $250.00 × 0.05 = $12.50. QST: $250.00 × 0.09975 = $24.94. Total tax: $37.43.

What If You're a Business? (Input Tax Credits and ITRs)

If your business is registered for GST, you may be able to claim Input Tax Credits (ITCs) to recover the 5% GST paid on eligible business expenses. If you are registered for QST, you may be able to claim Input Tax Refunds (ITRs) to recover the 9.975% QST. Eligibility depends on your registration status and the nature of the expense — consult a tax professional for advice specific to your situation.

Quebec Sales Tax Exemptions

Not every Quebec purchase includes GST and QST. Common exempt and zero-rated items include:

  • Basic groceries
  • Prescription drugs
  • Most medical devices
  • Books (QST-exempt only; GST still applies)

If an item is exempt, there is no tax to back out — do not apply the ÷ 1.14975 formula to those totals.
